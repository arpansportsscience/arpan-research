About Me

Hello, I am Arpan Chattopadhyay, a PhD research scholar from the Central University of Rajasthan. My research focuses on sports injury, sports injury biomarkers (Cell free circulatory mitochondrial DNA), and exercise physiology.

I am dedicated to advancing knowledge in Sports Science through rigorous research and molecular analysis. Specifically, I am interested in understanding the underlying mechanisms of sports injuries and identifying biomarkers that can be used for early detection and prevention.

Research Focus
  • Sports Injury Science
  • Exercise Physiology
  • CFC-MT-DNA Biomarkers
  • Athlete Recovery
