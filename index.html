About Me

I am a PhD research scholar at the Central University of Rajasthan. My expertise lies in evaluating cell-free circulatory mitochondrial DNA (cf-mtDNA) as a biomarker for physiological and psychological stress.

My work aims to bridge the gap between molecular bioscience and applied sports medicine to improve athlete health monitoring.
