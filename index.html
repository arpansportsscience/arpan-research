About My Research

I am a PhD Scholar at the Central University of Rajasthan. My work stands at the intersection of Molecular Biology and Sports Medicine.

I specialize in Cell-free circulating mitochondrial DNA (CFC-MT-DNA). By analyzing these tiny genetic fragments in athletes, we can understand how the body responds to high-intensity stress and prevent injuries before they happen.
